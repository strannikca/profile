Hi there!

This page is intended to provide some informal information about me as a person.

You are welcome to make a quick jump to a page about one of my specializations or use footer link to download my resume. At the current moment the site is not finished completely and has only web-development specialization page.

My name is Alex. I was born and grew up in Ukraine. As most of software engineers, my first programming attempts started as soon as I got my first PC in 1994, and first website development experience as soon as I was connected to the Internet in 1998. The first time I was paid for software development in 2001 - it is the point I count my professional experience from, so it is more than 15 years for now. During all these years, I specialized mostly in two subfields: web development and application development for Windows platform. In 2012 I decided to try some mobile app development, however I did it for Windows Phone platform exclusively, which is totally ignored here in Canada, so I do not mention about this much. Actually, I shipped two small pilot apps and they even had some income. My third one was intended to bring up success to the next level, but I reviewed my strategy and decided to switch to game development after moving to Canada in late 2015. By this I tried to embody my long-lasting passion to create games and started to develop my own project. Unfortunately, I underestimated all the challenges and mostly all the roles I should have covered by myself both in development process and doing marketing and management, so that my time-money resource ran out long before the planned project stage was achieved. That is why this project is now indefinitely frozen.

One more item I think is worth to be covered here - the absence of portfolio. If fact this is the main reason for creating this demo site. The problem is that most of the work I did was under NDA. On the other hand, all side projects I remember as well as my own ones are all dead. And I was so excited going to the new life to Canada... with this slogan erased all the old archives.
